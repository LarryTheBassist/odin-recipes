Cape Cod

Description

This Cape Cod drink is made with vodka and cranberry juice for a deliciously refreshing cocktail.

Ingredients

  • ice cubes
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • ⅓ cup cranberry juice
  • 1 wedge lime

Steps

  • Place ice in a short tumbler or old fashioned glass; pour in vodka and cranberry juice. Squeeze lime wedge into drink, then add it to the glass. Serve.
